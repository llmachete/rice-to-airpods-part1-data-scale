## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Search Strategy
1. **Primary Search:** "[Measurement] + volume + cubic meters + official/standard"
2. **Authority Search:** "[Measurement] + [governing body] + specifications"
3. **Verification Search:** "[Measurement] + dimensions + authoritative source"

### Verification Process
1. Cross-reference multiple sources for volume data
2. Verify unit conversions independently
3. Check source authority and publication date
4. Confirm measurements match JSON values
5. Document discrepancies and flag for review

### Source Prioritization
1. Government agencies and standards bodies (highest priority)
2. Academic and research institutions
3. International standards organizations
4. Industry standards and trade associations
5. Reputable reference databases
6. Encyclopedia and secondary sources (last resort)
